Southwestern Mac and Cheese

This gooey mac and cheese makes a fantastic meal any time of day

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of elbow macaroni
  • 3 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 1 small yellow onion (diced)
  • 2 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 teaspoons of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of half and half
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 2 cups of colby jack cheese (shredded)
  • 15 ounces of diced tomatoes (drained)
  • 4 ounces of green chiles (drained)
  • 15 ounces of black beans (drained and rinsed)
  • 1/3 cup of frozen corn
